- The Matching Game consists of a board containing multiple cells 
that represent characters or letters.

- The objective of the player is to select two cells with matching 
characters or letters that can be connected by 1 of 4 patterns: 
I, L, U, and Z.

- Validly connected shapes will make the two cells disappear.

- The game will end if the player clears all the cells on the board 
or runs out of the provided number of lives.

        - Right matching:   +20 pts
        - Wrong matching:   -5 pts, -1 live
        - Move suggestion:  -5 pts
